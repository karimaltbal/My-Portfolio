Create your schedule system

2021

Web Development & Coding Certificate

-the problem:
There are some colleges in Egypt based on the credit hour system, which is a system whose idea is based on that the student creates a schedule to record his courses through the many schedules offered by the college. Through these schedules, the student can create his own schedule based on (number of subjects - type of doctor). And the teaching assistant he wants - choosing the dates of each subject for him) and this is a difficult matter for a student To get to the right table.
-The solution:
The system facilitates this process and saves time and effort, and that is done by simply entering the schedules imposed by the college, from which the student chooses (the materials to be registered) and the system will provide the schedule for the student with all the possibilities, some of which the student can print and register.
